About Eyesight Collective

Hearing a story from someone who you thought was more successful than you and recognizing a bit of yourself in that person has the power to create the next generation of change makers and leaders.


Eyesight Collective is the brainchild of college students Jake Hurwitz and Nathan Moses. It is a multi-dimensional company focused on helping students and young professionals turn their passions into their careers and thrive along the way, while recognizing that the journey is the reward. Through weekly videos, blogs, webinars and in-person workshops, the Eyesight community exists to bring together mentors and mentees, while focusing on teaching the soft-skills that are needed to compete in today’s extremely competitive business environment.


We are committed to impacting the lives of thousands of students through storytelling and mentorship, opening their eyes to a world of possibility that they at first didn’t even know existed.
